    EACT in Action - Online Payment Fraud: How Confirmation of Payee can help corporate treasurers

    Hosted by

    EACT

    Thanks to all who joined us for the latest EACT in Action webinar, providing an in-depth examination of Confirmation of Payee (CoP) and the practical ways in which it can help corporate treasurers on a day-to-day basis.

    A recording of the session is available below.

     

    Watch out for further EACT in Action events over the course of the coming months covering priority issues for the treasury community.

     

     

    Speakers

    		    


    Wiebe Fokma
    Director EMEA, Global Advisory, BioCatch

    Wiebe is part of BioCatch's Global Advisory Team, which advises on the use of endpoint user behavior to find new attack methods and use cases in banking and finance. He has worked in and for major banks since 1999, spending the last 12 years working in fraud prevention and AML. Through his experience, Wiebe realized that transaction-based AI models require Behavioral Insights (behavioral analysis) of bank customers, so he joined BioCatch in 2022 to further develop their capabilities.


    Bob Ford
    Director, Payments SME Ltd

    Bob is a long-standing practitioner in the world of banking payments. Expertise gained at leading financial institutions including Citi, Barclays, Fortis and Deutsche. Technology companies including Cable & Wireless, SwallowTech and payment schemes including BACS and Faster Payments. This experience gained across multiple payment programmes both in the UK and Europe.

    Bob is currently researching the expansion capabilities for Confirmation of Payee to provide concepts around the handling the money mules and how to expand the data aspects of confirmation of payee to assist in paying bank be able to detect fraud scams earlier, before funds are actually moved.

     


    Naresh Aggarwal
    Associate Director – Policy and Technical, ACT

    Naresh is a member of the Policy and Technical team at the Association of Corporate Treasurers. One of his areas of focus is payments and he regularly meets with the payments team at the Bank of England and has been a member of FCA and pay.uk panels (including one on Confirmation of Payee). Prior to joining the ACT, Naresh was a treasury consultant with PwC and led cash management and payments for the firm. Additionally he brings 20 years of operational treasury experience and has advised a FinTech on their regulatory payment authorisation.

    Moderator

    		   


    John Bertrand
    Director, On Demand Payment Technologies

    John blends banking with technology to create value and safety for customers. Expertise gained at Citi, Misys, FISglobal, SAP and FinTech in the UK and USA. Managed Cash Management and grew transaction banking by creating strong customer loyalty with Corporate Treasurers.

    Worked with the Bank of England the Federal Reserve regulators. Completed three consultative papers for The Payment Service Regulatory on Applied Push Payment Scams, Confirmation of Payee and reimbursements.

    Liveryman of The City of London, M.B.A. Warwick University and B.Sc.(Hons) City University (Engineering). Author four books, including Liquidity Strategies for Corporates and Open Banking. Supports, for his sins, Northampton Saints.
