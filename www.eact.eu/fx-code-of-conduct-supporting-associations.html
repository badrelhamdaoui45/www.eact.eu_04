    Supporting EACT Associations

     

    The following associations, members of the EACT, support adherence to the Code by the FX market and are promoting the Code to their members, by encouraging them to evolve their institutions’ FX practices to be consistent with the principles in the Code:

    • ACT – Association of Corporate Treasurers
    • ACTA - Austrian Corporate Treasury Association
    • ACTSR – Association of Corporate Treasurers  Suisse Romande
    • AFTE - Association Française des Trésoriers d’Entreprise
    • ASSET – Asociación Española de Financieros y Tesoreros de Empresa
    • ATEB – Association of Treasury Experts in Belgium
    • ATEL – Luxembourg Association of Corporate Treasurers
    • CACT – Croation Association of Corporate Treasurers
    • CAT – Ceska Asociace Treasury
    • HTC – Hungarian Treasury Club
    • IACT – Irish Association of Corporate Treasurers
    • PCTA – Polish Corporate Treasurers Association
    • SAF – Slovak Association of Finance and TreasurySAF – Slovak Association of Finance and Treasury
    • VDT - Verband Deutscher Treasurer”
