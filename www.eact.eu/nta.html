    Suspension of Membership of EACT Russian Member, i.e. RACT

    Given the actions of Russia against Ukraine and in consistency with wider EU sanctions, EACT feels that we need to take action, both from the perspective of adherence to current and possible wider sanctions and to act in the best interests of our membership. The EACT Board has therefore decided to temporarily suspend its Russian Member until further notice, with immediate effect. We will keep under review external developments with a view to consider the affected Member’s status.
